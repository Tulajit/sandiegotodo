Day Trips from San Diego

Mexico in 30 minutes. Desert wilderness in 90. Wine country in an hour. Mountains, national parks, and beach towns — San Diego is the perfect base for Southern California exploration.

San Diego's location in the southwestern corner of the continental US puts it within striking distance of an extraordinary range of landscapes and experiences. In under three hours you can be in Mexico, the Mojave Desert, wine country, mountain forests, or downtown Los Angeles. Few cities in America offer this kind of day trip diversity.

Day trips FAQs

What are the best day trips from San Diego?
Tijuana (30 min), Anza-Borrego Desert (90 min), Temecula Wine Country (1 hr), Joshua Tree (2.5 hrs), and Los Angeles (2.5 hrs) are among the best day trips from San Diego.
Can you do a day trip to Tijuana from San Diego?
Yes — Tijuana is just 30 minutes from downtown. Park on the US side at San Ysidro and walk across. Bring your passport. The food scene alone is worth the trip.
How far is Joshua Tree from San Diego?
Joshua Tree National Park is approximately 2.5 hours (150 miles) from San Diego. Leave early, spend 4-5 hours in the park, and return in the evening for a full day trip.
