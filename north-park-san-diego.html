Neighborhood Guide

North Park

San Diego's craft beer capital and hippest neighborhood — breweries, vinyl shops, taquerias, and an arts scene that keeps getting better.

North Park is where San Diego locals actually live and hang out. This walkable urban neighborhood just north of Balboa Park has evolved over the past decade into one of the best food and drink destinations in Southern California. The 30th Street corridor is the craft beer heart of the city, University Avenue buzzes with restaurants from every cuisine imaginable, and the arts scene brings a creative energy that's entirely its own.

Craft beer

Best breweries in North Park

Brewery
Modern Times Beer
One of San Diego's most acclaimed craft breweries with a beautiful tasting room. Known for creative IPAs, stouts, and their outstanding coffee program.
Brewery
Mike Hess Brewing
North Park institution with a rotating tap list, spacious outdoor patio, and a welcoming neighborhood vibe. Great for afternoon beer flights.
Brewery
Thorn Street Brewery
Small-batch neighborhood brewery with outstanding lagers, sours, and the most laid-back patio in North Park.
Brewery
North Park Beer Co.
Neighborhood favorite with a rooftop deck, excellent IPAs, and a food menu that rivals dedicated restaurants.
Food & drink

Where to eat in North Park

Tacos
Tacos El Franc
Authentic street tacos with handmade tortillas. The lengua (tongue) and birria tacos are outstanding. Open late on weekends.
Coffee
Communal Coffee
North Park's most beloved coffee shop — excellent single-origin roasts, beautiful space, and the best avocado toast in the neighborhood.
Burgers
Small Bar
Intimate craft cocktail bar with an exceptional burger menu. The double smash burger is one of the best in San Diego.
Vietnamese
Pho King Delicious
Outstanding Vietnamese food in a casual, unpretentious setting. The pho broth is made fresh daily — order the large.
North Park local tips
  • Thursday night is the best night in North Park — less crowded than weekends but the full energy of the neighborhood is on display.
  • The North Park Farmers Market runs every Thursday evening — produce, food trucks, live music, and a great neighborhood atmosphere.
  • Park once and walk — the commercial strips on 30th Street and University Avenue are easy to navigate on foot.
  • Pigment and Found on 30th are two of the best independent home goods and gift shops in San Diego.
  • The Observatory North Park is one of SD's best live music venues — check their calendar before visiting.

North Park FAQs

What is North Park San Diego known for?
North Park is known as San Diego's craft beer capital. It's home to dozens of craft breweries, an eclectic food scene, vintage shops, and independent coffee shops.
Is North Park walkable?
Yes, North Park is one of San Diego's most walkable neighborhoods. University Avenue and 30th Street are easy to navigate on foot with restaurants, bars, and breweries clustered close together.
What is the best brewery in North Park?
Modern Times Beer, Mike Hess Brewing, and Thorn Street Brewery are consistently rated among the best in North Park.
