About SanDiegoTodo.com

SanDiegoTodo.com is an independent local guide to San Diego — written by people who actually live here, updated constantly, and never sponsored by the tourist board.

We started this site because we kept seeing the same recycled tourist content about San Diego online — the same 10 attractions, the same stock photos, the same advice that anyone could have written without ever visiting. We wanted something better: a resource that reflects what San Diego is actually like to live in and explore.

What we cover

Everything worth doing in San Diego — beaches, hiking trails, restaurants, bars, neighborhoods, day trips, family activities, arts and culture, and more. We cover well-known spots honestly and dig up hidden gems that most visitors never find.

Our approach

We visit every place we write about. We don't accept payment for positive coverage. We update our content regularly as things change. We write for people who want real information, not marketing copy.

Who we are

A small team of San Diego locals — writers, photographers, and people who spend too much time exploring their own city. We live in neighborhoods across San Diego from Ocean Beach to North Park to La Jolla, which gives us a genuinely diverse perspective on what the city has to offer.
