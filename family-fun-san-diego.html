Family Fun in San Diego

World-class zoos, calm family beaches, hands-on museums, theme parks, and 266 sunny days a year — San Diego is one of America's best family destinations.

San Diego consistently ranks among the top family vacation destinations in the United States — and it earns the ranking. The combination of world-class attractions, calm family beaches, year-round mild weather, and a wealth of free and affordable activities makes it genuinely hard to run out of things to do with kids of any age.

Top family attractions

Best activities for families in San Diego

🦁
All ages
San Diego Zoo
One of the world's best zoos — 3,500 animals, 100 acres, and exhibits that put animals in immersive natural habitats. Plan a full day. The Skyfari aerial tram gives great views across the park.
🌊
All ages
SeaWorld San Diego
Marine life shows, dolphin encounters, roller coasters, and the best orca experience in California. The Penguin Encounter and shark exhibit are standouts. Full day activity.
🧱
Ages 2–12
Legoland California
30 miles north in Carlsbad — an entire theme park built for younger children. Miniland USA (US landmarks in Lego) is extraordinary. The water park add-on is excellent in summer.
🏖️
All ages
La Jolla Shores
San Diego's best family beach — calm waves, wide sandy shore, lifeguards on duty, grassy picnic area with BBQ grills. Rentals for kayaks and paddleboards available on the beach.
🔬
Ages 5+
Fleet Science Center
Hands-on science museum in Balboa Park with interactive exhibits and an IMAX theater. The Tinkering Studio lets kids build and experiment. One of the best children's museums in California.
🚢
Ages 6+
USS Midway Museum
Explore one of the world's largest naval museums — an actual aircraft carrier docked on the harbor. Kids can sit in cockpits, tour the ship, and watch flight simulators. Genuinely exciting for all ages.
🦈
All ages
Birch Aquarium at Scripps
World-class oceanographic aquarium in La Jolla run by Scripps Institution of Oceanography. The kelp forest tank, seahorse exhibit, and touch tide pools are highlights. Reasonable admission, outstanding quality.
🎡
All ages
Belmont Park
Historic amusement park on Mission Beach — the classic Giant Dipper wooden roller coaster has been running since 1925. Arcade, mini golf, and beach access right next door. Perfect for a half-day visit.

San Diego family FAQs

What are the best family activities in San Diego?
The San Diego Zoo, La Jolla Shores beach, SeaWorld, Legoland California, the USS Midway Museum, and the Birch Aquarium are consistently rated the best family activities in San Diego.
Is San Diego good for families with young children?
San Diego is one of the best family vacation destinations in America. World-class attractions, calm family beaches, and mild weather make it ideal for children of all ages.
What is free to do with kids in San Diego?
Balboa Park gardens, all San Diego beaches, tide pools at Cabrillo National Monument, and Sunday organ concerts at Spreckels Pavilion are all free or very low cost.
Plan your San Diego family trip
From beaches to zoos — San Diego has everything for an unforgettable family vacation.
